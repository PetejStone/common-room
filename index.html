About Us

The Common Room is a welcoming and worshipping community of college aged students and young adults from all over Lewis County. We gather on Tuesday evenings to:

  • Learn about and worship the living God
  • Courageously seek to know Jesus and ourselves
  • Spend time time in fellowship talking, laughing and eating

Tuesday

We meet on Tuesday nights for worship and a message, followed by fellowship, pizza, and games. Come join our loving community of young adults!

Giving

The generous gifts from the church body are used to support our ministries and operations, and support the spreading of the gospel by expanding the kingdom.

There is a tithing basket available in the back during service. We also make tithing available through Easy Tithe - a safe, secure online portal for giving.
